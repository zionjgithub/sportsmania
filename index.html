NFl : news and rumors

News

The San Francisco 49ers have arguably the best roster in the NFL. They are loaded on both sides of the ball with All-Pros and players with the largest contracts at their positions in the NFL, as the team gets ready to make another run at the Super Bowl. But, even the best teams have some holes that need filling. San Francisco addressed arguably their biggest need very early in free agency. After getting gashed by the Philadelphia Eagles in the NFC Championship Game, the 49ers made a big upgrade on the interior of their defensive line, signing Javon Hargrave. After ranking as the No. 1 defense in the 2022 season, it is certainly surprising to see all of their needs be on that side of the ball. Even with the addition of Hargrave, the defense could use a little more punch in the pass-rushing department.
